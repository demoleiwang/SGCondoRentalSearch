## Context

- Household: couple in their early 30s with a 5-year-old son and a baby on the way.
- Incomes: husband SGD 95k, wife SGD 65k. Combined take-home ~$10,500/mo.
- Lease ending in 3 months; looking to move before the baby comes.
- Target unit: 4-room HDB resale rental OR 3BR condo in the $3,500–$4,500 range; prefer HDB for value.
- Primary school catchment: the 5-year-old is registered at a specific Tampines primary school — **location relative to that school matters more than MRT**.
- Work: both commute to Changi Business Park by car (~15 min).
- Own one car. MRT is nice-to-have, not blocking.
- Prefers east side: Tampines / Pasir Ris / Bedok. Will consider Simei / Pasir Ris West if cheap.

## Search Style

- English-only, plain natural language. No slang, no code-speak.
- Phrases the query by need, not by field: "3 bedroom HDB tampines near primary school", "family condo east side kids".
- Will type the school name directly if it doesn't return useful results: "near Red Swastika primary".
- Hates long lists — wants 5–10 solid options, not 40.
- Will abandon and go to PropertyGuru if the first 5 look wrong.

## Success Criteria

1. HDB 4-room results are first-class — not buried under condos.
2. Can specify "near school X" or at minimum filter by postal code / district / town.
3. Commute-by-car is an option (or at least not penalized against).
4. Enough context per result to tell "would this fit a family of 4 with a toddler?"

## Quit Triggers

- Default sort buries HDB options.
- Nothing about schools, childcare, parks, or family-friendly neighborhoods.
- Condos that clearly cater to singles/couples dominate the top results when "HDB" is specified.
- No way to express "we drive, MRT distance doesn't matter as much as school distance".
