### Lesson

Synthetic-Marcus confirmed: a condo search tool aimed at salaried professionals with a fixed workplace and lifestyle preferences needs three things the current UI lacks — integrated door-to-door commute minutes, lifestyle amenity signals per project, and the P25 bargaining target surfaced at row level. Without those, the value over raw PropertyGuru filtering is mostly the landmark-expansion and data.gov baselines.

## Findings

- **(P0)** `F001` No lifestyle amenity signal per project — gym, cafe, supermarket within 10-min walk nowhere shown. Marcus can't judge whether LUMIERE or ICON would give him the lifestyle he wants. Repro: `2b2b near OUE Downtown 4500`.
- **(P1)** `F002` Commute time to the workplace (OUE Downtown) is missing — only walking distance to nearest MRT is shown. For anyone searching by landmark, the relevant number is door-to-door to that landmark. Repro: `2b2b near OUE Downtown 4500`.
- **(P1)** `F003` P25 negotiation target is buried — hidden in a collapsed per-project "Rent Trend" panel. For price-conscious users this is the single most useful number and belongs at the row. Repro: `Tanjong Pagar 2 bedroom 4000-5000`.
- **(P1)** `F004` Smart-search radius includes 1088m-away projects without a user control to tighten to walkable-only. The result mixes "3-min walk" condos with "13-min walk" ones in the same ranking. Repro: `2b2b near OUE Downtown 4500`.
- **(P2)** `F005` Result rows lack floor / facing / sqft / facilities / year-built fields — forcing a PropertyGuru click per candidate to make any meaningful triage. Repro: `OUE Downtown One 2房 4500以内`.
